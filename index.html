About OrigoAid

Born in Bangladesh, built for Bangladesh — empowering communities through the power of collective giving

Our Mission

Bridging the Gap Between Generosity and Need

OrigoAid was founded with one simple belief: no Bangladeshi should be denied medical care, education, or recovery from disaster simply because of financial barriers.

We provide a trusted, transparent platform where fundraisers can tell their stories and donors can give with confidence — knowing every taka reaches its intended purpose.

From flood victims in Sylhet to students in Rangpur seeking scholarships, OrigoAid is the connective tissue between Bangladesh's generous hearts.
